# Image Prompts for Radha Krishna Stories

Use these prompts to generate high-quality images for the Radha Krishna Katha section. Copy and paste them into Gemini or Midjourney.

## General Style Note

Add this suffix to all prompts for consistent style:

> **Style:** Digital art, hyper-realistic, Indian mythological art style, soft divine lighting using bioluminescent effects, vibrant colors (Peacock Blue, Pink, Gold), intricate jewelry and clothing details, 8k resolution, cinematic composition, ethereal atmosphere.

---

## Story Prompts

### 1. Birth of Krishna (`/RadhaKrishna/birth.png`)

**Prompt:** baby Krishna being carried in a basket by Vasudeva across a raging Yamuna river at midnight during a heavy storm with lightning. The river waters are miraculously parting to make a path. A multi-headed serpent (Sheshnag) uses its hoods as an umbrella to protect the baby from rain. Dark blue atmospheric lighting with a divine glow around the baby.

### 2. Childhood in Gokul (`/RadhaKrishna/childhood.png`)

**Prompt:** Toddler Krishna crawling in a beautiful courtyard in Gokul, wearing yellow silk (dhoti) and peacock feather. He is surrounded by happy cows and calves. Mother Yashoda is looking at him with immense love. Soft sunlight filtering through trees, lush green background, peaceful village atmosphere.

### 3. Krishna the Butter Thief (`/RadhaKrishna/butter-thief.png`)

**Prompt:** Young Krishna standing on a wooden stool or his friends' shoulders, reaching for a hanging pot of butter (dahi handi) in a rustic village kitchen. He has butter smeared on his face and hands. A monkey is sitting nearby, also eating butter. The scene is playful and mischievous, warm indoor lighting.

### 4. Meeting Radha (`/RadhaKrishna/meeting-radha.png`)

**Prompt:** First meeting of Radha and Krishna in a beautiful flowering grove in Vrindavan. Krishna is blue-skinned playing a flute, Radha is fair-skinned looking at him with devotion. They are surrounded by colorful flowers, peacocks, and gentle deer. Soft romantic pink and blue lighting, magical atmosphere.

### 5. Flute of Love (`/RadhaKrishna/flute.png`)

**Prompt:** Krishna playing his flute (bansuri) under a Kadamba tree on the banks of the Yamuna river at sunset. The water is sparkling gold. Peacocks are dancing, cows are listening mesmerized. The air is filled with magical musical notes visualized as glowing sparkles. Serene and melodious composition.

### 6. Raas Leela (`/RadhaKrishna/raas-leela.png`)

**Prompt:** A magnificent night scene of Raas Leela under a full moon. Krishna has multiplied himself and is dancing with many Gopis in a circle. Radha and Krishna are in the center dancing. Bioluminescent lotus flowers in the water, starry night sky, ethereal blue and silver moonlight. Dynamic dancing poses.

### 7. Krishna Lifts Govardhan (`/RadhaKrishna/govardhan.png`)

**Prompt:** Seven-year-old Krishna lifting the massive Govardhan Hill effortlessly on the little finger of his left hand. Under the hill, villagers and cows are taking shelter from a terrible thunderstorm with heavy rain and lightning outside. Krishna is glowing, protecting everyone. Dramatic contrast between the dark storm outside and the warm safety inside.

### 8. Defeating Kaliya (`/RadhaKrishna/kaliya.png`)

**Prompt:** Young Krishna dancing fearlessly on the many hoods of a giant, venomous black serpent (Kaliya) in the middle of a turbulent river. The serpent looks subdued. Water splashes around them. Dramatic action shot, powerful stance of Krishna.

### 9. Love of Radha (`/RadhaKrishna/radha-love.png`)

**Prompt:** Radha sitting alone in a beautiful garden, lost in deep meditation/thought of Krishna. She is glowing with an inner light of devotion. A translucent image or vision of Krishna is visible in her heart or next to her. Soft, emotional, spiritual atmosphere.

### 10. Farewell from Vrindavan (`/RadhaKrishna/farewell.png`)

**Prompt:** A heartbreaking scene of Krishna on a chariot leaving Vrindavan. Radha stands silently weeping in the background. Krishna looks back with a tear in his eye. Gopis are trying to stop the chariot wheels. Sunset or twilight setting symbolizing separation. Emotional and dramatic.

### 11. Krishna Defeats Kansa (`/RadhaKrishna/kansa.png`)

**Prompt:** Krishna in a royal wrestling arena in Mathura, pulling the tyrant King Kansa down from his throne by his hair. Kansa looks terrified. The crowd watches in awe. Powerful, dynamic action pose. Ancient Indian palace architecture in the background.

### 12. Krishna the King (`/RadhaKrishna/king.png`)

**Prompt:** Adult Lord Krishna sitting on a magnificent golden throne in the city of Dwarka. He wears a royal crown with a peacock feather and rich jewelry. The architecture involves gold and crystal pillars with the ocean visible in the background. Majestic, royal, and calm demeanor.

### 13. Radha’s Devotion (`/RadhaKrishna/radha-devotion.png`)

**Prompt:** Radha worshiping a clay idol of Krishna in Vrindavan with flowers and a lamp (diya). Simple village setting but the atmosphere is glowing with divine spiritual energy. Her expression is one of pure surrender and bliss.

### 14. Krishna and Sudama (`/RadhaKrishna/sudama.png`)

**Prompt:** King Krishna sitting on his throne, washing the feet of his poor, ragged friend Sudama with water. Sudama looks shy and overwhelmed. Rukmini is fanning them. The contrast between Krishna's royal attire and Sudama's torn clothes highlights the bond of friendship. Warm, emotional lighting.

### 15. Krishna as a Guide (Gita) (`/RadhaKrishna/guide.png`)

**Prompt:** The battlefield of Kurukshetra. Krishna as the charioteer driving a chariot with four white horses. Arjuna is sitting behind him, listening intently. Krishna is speaking the Gita. A divine cosmic aura or Vishwaroop form is faintly visible in the sky behind Krishna. Epic, cinematic war setting.

### 16. Radha Krishna’s Spiritual Love (`/RadhaKrishna/spiritual-love.png`)

**Prompt:** An abstract or symbolic representation of Radha and Krishna merging into one divine light. Half Krishna (blue) and half Radha (fair) or two souls intertwining. Cosmic background with galaxies and stars. Mystical and ethereal.

### 17. Krishna the Protector (`/RadhaKrishna/protector.png`)

**Prompt:** Krishna saving Draupadi in the court assembly. A stream of colorful endless saree fabric is flowing from Krishna's hand to protect Draupadi, who is praying with eyes closed. The court members look astonished. A scene of divine intervention and miracle.

### 18. Eternal Bond in Goloka (`/RadhaKrishna/eternal-bond.png`)

**Prompt:** Radha and Krishna sitting on a swing (jhula) decorated with flowers in the eternal realm of Goloka Vrindavan. The background is a paradise with wish-fulfilling trees and distinct golden light. They are looking into each other's eyes. Pure bliss and eternity.

### 19. Symbol of Divine Union (`/RadhaKrishna/symbol.png`)

**Prompt:** Artistic symbol of the 'Om' or a Lotus flower, where Radha and Krishna are depicted within the petals. Intricate mandala art style. Glowing colors of Blue, Pink, and Gold. Symmetrical and meditative design.

### 20. Legacy of Divine Love (`/RadhaKrishna/legacy.png`)

**Prompt:** A collage or montage showing people from different eras singing and dancing in kirtan (chanting Hare Krishna). The spirit of Radha Krishna hovering above them in the sky, blessing them. Joyful, musical, and celebratory atmosphere.
